    Overview

    Experience the future of on-device AI with the DEEPX DX-M1M. This AI processor delivers a stunning 25 TOPS while maintaining an ultra-low power consumption of just 3W(Typical). We prioritize Inferences Per Second (IPS) per Watt, so every operation delivers maximum, real-world value.

     

    The DX-M1M integrates effortlessly with your host system, accelerating diverse applications like computer vision and audio analysis.

     

    We believe that powerful AI should be accessible to all. Simply use your models from popular frameworks like PyTorch or ONNX, and our tools will automatically optimize them for the DX-M1M. This allows you to focus on innovation, not integration.

    Key Benefits

    Unprecedented Power EfficiencyThe DX-M1M is a groundbreaking AI processor that redefines on-device intelligence. It delivers a powerful 25 TOPS in a 3W ultra-low power design, prioritizing Inferences Per Second (IPS) per Watt for maximum, real-world value. It's the perfect solution for power-sensitive edge devices.

    A Seamless Developer ExperienceWe believe that powerful AI should be easy to use. The DX-M1M comes with a comprehensive software environment. This tool automatically optimizes models from popular frameworks like PyTorch and ONNX, so you can focus on innovation, not complex integration.

    Innovative, AI-Optimized ArchitectureThe DX-M1M features a dedicated NPU (Neural Processing Unit), an architecture built specifically to accelerate deep learning. Its specialized hardware for common AI tasks like pooling and Softmax enables faster, more efficient processing and true real-time on-device AI.

    Robust, Hardware-Based SecurityProtecting your AI is our priority. DX-M1M’s robust, hardware-based security system features a Root of Trust and a True Random Number Generator (TRNG), ensuring your AI models and data are protected from a wide range of external threats.
