    What is the DX TechBridge Program ?

    DEEPX’s Technology Adoption Framework offers two support options: The ‘DX TechBridge Program’ for early-stage integration and the ‘Mass Production Plan’ for full-scale development. Both deliver tailored technical support, resources, and tools to help customers advance from initial exploration to mass manufacturing.

    Option A

    DX TechBridge Program

    Start your AI journey with ease and accelerate innovation. The DX TechBridge Program provides developers and innovators with essential tools, documentation, and dedicated technical support. It’s designed to help you swiftly prototype and test AI solutions using DEEPX NPU technology, minimizing hurdles and accelerating your time-to-market.

      Frequently Asked Questions

      What happens after I submit my application?
      Once you submit your application, DEEPX will review your project to assess its suitability for the plan. If approved, we will contact you to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA). After signing the NDA, you will receive the necessary AI chips and evaluation kits, along with dedicated technical support.
      Can I change my plan option after applying?
      Yes, you can modify your plan option at any time. To request changes, please contact your assigned sales representative or email sales@deepx.ai. We will assist you promptly.
      What are the differences between the Basic and Pro plans?
      Basic: Up to 3 hours of technical support, automated SDK, and basic model deployment support.
      Pro: Up to 1 week (7 days) of technical support, automated SDK with optimization, enhanced model performance and accuracy, and additional compiler and runtime support.
      How can I get information about plan costs and additional technical support?
      Plan costs vary depending on the selected option and required level of support. After submitting your application, your assigned sales representative will provide detailed pricing and support information.
      Where can I ask additional questions or make inquiries?
      For further inquiries or special requests, please email sales@deepx.ai or fill out the inquiry form on our website. Our team will respond as quickly as possible.
      Do plan participants get priority access to DEEPX’s latest products?
      Yes, Evaluation Kit plan participants receive early access to DEEPX’s latest AI chips and technologies for testing and evaluation.
      How long does the plan last?
      The duration varies based on the selected plan option and project requirements. The Basic plan is designed for short-term evaluations, while the Pro plan supports more in-depth, long-term engagements.
      Does the plan offer marketing support or collaboration opportunities?
      Yes, in addition to technical support, the Evaluation Kit plan provides opportunities for marketing collaborations, joint promotions, and potential partnerships. For more details, please contact your sales representative.
      What types of AI models are compatible with DEEPX AI chips?
      DEEPX AI chips support a wide range of AI models, including those for image processing, video analysis, and speech recognition, among other applications.
      How can I evaluate the performance of DEEPX AI chips?
      Participants receive an evaluation kit along with technical support, enabling them to test and validate the performance and efficiency of DEEPX AI chips in real-world scenarios.
      Any Questions?
      For inquiries about our products and services, please contact us.
