    Overview

    Integrated decoding, encoding, and transcoding turn the DX-H1 V-NPU into a complete foundation for real-time, large-scale monitoring. What once required rooms full of expensive, power-hungry servers can now be placed into the hands of any organization. The barrier to advanced AI falls away, and what remains is possibility, accessible, sustainable, and inevitable.

    Key Benefits

    Play Video

    Revolutionary TCO82% lower hardware costs plus 85% electricity cost savings (1,000-channel system, 5-year basis). Transform expensive multi-server GPU setups into single, efficient edge cards accessible to any organization.

    All-In-One ArchitectureDual hardware video codecs with dual NPUs handle complete video processing pipeline on a single card. This eliminates separate video decoders, minimizes CPU overhead on core systems, and ensures stable 24/7 operation without performance degradation.

    Smart Monitoring SystemAdvanced edge processing analyzes video locally and transmits only critical events to VMS systems, drastically reducing bandwidth and storage costs. V-NPU's state-of-the-art AI models support enable natural language video search that transforms days of manual review into seconds of instant discovery.

    Effortless ScalabilityCompact design without fan enables high-density deployments impossible with bulky GPUs. Deploy up to 4 cards per 2U server (256 channels) for maximum rack space efficiency and rapid scaling with existing infrastructure.
