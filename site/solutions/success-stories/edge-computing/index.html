    Edge Computing:
    Meaning and Market Shift

    For years, computing has been centralized — workloads were pushed to the cloud for processing, while devices at the edge acted only as simple data collectors. This model, while effective for many applications, has shown serious weaknesses in latency, reliability, cost, and energy efficiency.

     

    Edge Computing represents a decisive shift: bringing intelligence closer to where data is generated — at the device level, in real time, without relying on continuous cloud connectivity. It enables instant insights, improved data privacy, and dramatically reduced costs.

     

    At DEEPX, we believe Edge Computing is not just an extension of the cloud; it is the new foundation of the AI economy. Our NPUs are purpose-built to turn cameras, IoT devices, robots, factory, drone, mobility and gateways into intelligent edge systems capable of running advanced AI models locally with ultra-low power.

    The DEEPX Advantage:
    Driving Intelligence to the Edge

    Where traditional edge deployments face bottlenecks — limited compute budgets, heat constraints, or the inability to support large AI models — DEEPX delivers a breakthrough:


    • Ultra-Low Power NPUs (sub-5W, fanless) that make high-performance AI practical in compact devices.
    • Scalable SDK & Toolchain for effortless ONNX/TensorFlow/PyTorch model integration.
    • Flexible Form Factors (M.2 modules, PCIe cards, SoMs) that fit seamlessly into gateways, embedded PCs, and IoT appliances.

    This foundation allows system integrators and developers to deploy real AI intelligence at the edge — reliably, affordably, and at scale.

    Empowering IoT Edge with Cellular Connectivity and Local AI

    The success of DEEPX and Sixfab demonstrates how Edge Computing is becoming the backbone of modern digital infrastructure. No longer are devices mere conduits for data; they are active, intelligent participants in real-time decision-making.

    As demand grows for smarter cities, resilient factories, and autonomous systems, DEEPX is providing the silicon intelligence that drives Edge Computing forward. Our partnership with Sixfab proves that when connectivity meets intelligence, the future of IoT becomes truly transformative.

    Sixfab, a global leader in IoT connectivity solutions, enables developers and enterprises to bring reliable cellular and LPWAN connectivity to Raspberry Pi, Jetson, and other edge platforms. Their mission is to make edge devices not only connected, but capable.

    Through the partnership of Sixfab and DEEPX, a new generation of intelligent IoT gateways is being realized:

    The Challenge

    IoT gateways today face two critical gaps:

     

    1. Connectivity without intelligence — devices can connect, but can’t process data locally.
    2. Cloud dependence — raw data must be transmitted to cloud servers, incurring bandwidth costs, latency, and reliability risks.

    The Solution

    By integrating DEEPX NPUs into Sixfab’s edge connectivity platforms, we merge always-on connectivity with real-time AI inference. This creates a unified-edge gateway that not only links sensors, cameras, and machines but also understands them directly.

    • A Sixfab 5G IoT Hat paired with a DEEPX DX-M1 module turns a Raspberry Pi into a low-cost but powerful AI gateway.
    • Data from sensors and cameras is analyzed on-device — for example, detecting anomalies in industrial equipment or recognizing vehicles in smart mobility use cases.
    • Only metadata and actionable events are transmitted over 5G/LPWAN to the cloud, reducing bandwidth and storage by up to 90%.

    Results & Impact

    By integrating DEEPX NPUs into Sixfab’s edge connectivity platforms, we merge always-on connectivity with real-time AI inference. This creates a unified-edge gateway that not only links sensors, cameras, and machines but also understands them directly.

    • Latency cut to near-zero: real-time analytics at the gateway.
    • Dramatically lower TCO: reduced data transfer and server costs.
    • Scalable deployments: lightweight, affordable platforms that can be rolled out across factories, cities, or fleets.


    This collaboration embodies the true promise of Edge Computing: connectivity empowered with intelligence. Sixfab ensures every device can connect, while DEEPX ensures every device can think.

    Conclusion: A New Edge Ecosystem

    The success of DEEPX and Sixfab demonstrates how Edge Computing is becoming the backbone of modern digital infrastructure. No longer are devices mere conduits for data; they are active, intelligent participants in real-time decision-making.

    As demand grows for smarter cities, resilient factories, and autonomous systems, DEEPX is providing the silicon intelligence that drives Edge Computing forward. Our partnership with Sixfab proves that when connectivity meets intelligence, the future of IoT becomes truly transformative.
